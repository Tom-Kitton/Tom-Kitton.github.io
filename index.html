Welcome to a Traveller's Way

Click here to read HOOK's MISCELLANY, an irreverent survey of the present condition of the lands of Chanchette, Du Riett and Du Tropf. Hook's Miscellany was presented and conceived as a satire on the legendarily pompous Gazette of the Queen's Chain, prepared annually by Tobias Alledora Gaesmeade for more than three decades during the reign of Queen Meriett VII.

Click here to read the descriptions of the many counties and divisions of the Queen's Chain. The diverse lands which make up our well-esteemed commonwealth are exceedingly various in their climates, histories and manners; bewware the traveller who confuses the politics of Laurence with those of La Rhenc! These informative pieces are culled from the better editions of the Gazette of The Queen's Chain, as well as the privately produced reminiscences of the more observant sort of traveller.

Click here to read the Gazette of Strangers, an official publication of Her Majesty's Division for Stranger's Affairs. It publicly notifies the arrival of new persons to the country of the borders and is available for perusal at all offices of state. The characters created by everyone who plays the Traveler's Way play-by-post game are recorded in this document for perpetuity.
